## Why Cerber Exists

Cerber Core was born from real pain points encountered while building SaaS applications with AI assistance:

- **Problem:** AI tools don't understand project boundaries
- **Result:** Schema drift, architecture violations, CI fail loops, production incidents
- **Solution:** Enforce a human-written contract (CERBER.md) as single source of truth

The tool is tested in production at Eliksir SaaS and has saved countless hours of debugging and prevented multiple production incidents.
